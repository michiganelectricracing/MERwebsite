About

Michigan Electric Racing is a student-run team that designs, builds, and tests a formula-style electric race car while utilizing and developing the top engineers and leaders at the University of Michigan.



Michigan Hybrid Racing

Michigan Hybrid Racing was founded in 2011. The mission of the team was to design, build, test, and finance our own high-performance hybrid-electric Formula-style race car. The team then comppeted at the Formula Hybrid competition at the New Hampshire International Speedway in the spring against other teams from all over the world. The team most recently placed 5th at the 2017 Formula Hybrid Competition

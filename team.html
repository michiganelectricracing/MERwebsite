The Team


Captains

The team captains are responsible for maintaining team schedule, leading the technical direction of the vehicle, and organizing team level events and meetings. Captains lead team meetings, manage the team budget, and handle the technical logistics.

Vehicle Dynamics and Chassis

Vehicle Dynamics and Chassis (VDC) is responsible for most of the mechanical aspects of the vehicle design: the chassis, suspension, steering, and pedal box. They spend many long hours on mills and lathes and do the majority of the welding for the team.

Driveline

Driveline is responsible for delivering mechanical power to the ground from the motors. They conduct vehicle simulations and design and manufacture the gearboxes, corners, and brakes. Driveline has expertise in CNC machining.

Composites

Composites is responsible for all vehicle aerodynamics, covering the vehicle, and cooling. They design and manufacture the front and rear wings, undertray, bulkhead, nose cone, cooling routing, and all other aerodynamic and carbon systems. In addition they are responsible for driver ergonomics, headrest, and seat.

EV Powertrain

Electric Vehicle Powertrain is responsible for power delivery to the motors and most of the safety systems. This includes high voltage system architecture, batteries, motor controllers, and the safety loop.

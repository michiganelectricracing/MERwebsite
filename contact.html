Students

Interested in joining the team? We are always looking for undergraduate and graduate students who are passionate about building racecars or are looking to gain more engineering experience.

Our team is one of the most multi-disciplinary enginereing design teams on campus. We have engineering students from a majority of the majors and have several memebers from outside of engineering. As a new member coming into our orgnaization, we will teach you everything you need to know to become a dedicated and active member of our team.

Email us at michiganelectricracing@umich.edu if you want to join our team. If there's something in particular on our car you want to work on, let us know and our captains will put you in contact with the division lead responsible.
